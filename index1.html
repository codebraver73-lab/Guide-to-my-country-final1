The Holy Roman Empire (962‚Äì1806):

  • In 962, Otto I became the first Holy Roman Emperor, marking the beginning of the Holy Roman Empire, a complex, decentralized state composed of hundreds of semi-independent principalities and cities.

  • The empire lasted for nearly 1,000 years but was politically weak and fragmented, especially compared to centralized states like France and England.
